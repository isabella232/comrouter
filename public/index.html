Use Cases

Want your applications and services to reach more people worldwide? Here's how Com Router can help you:



Messages

  • send sms/mms
  • receive sms/mms
  • bulk messages
  • route between your app and messages
  • control, supervise, ect.


Voice

  • receive calls
  • make calls
  • control your app via voice
  • combox, conference, ect.
  • local numbers available in almost every country


SIP

  • Your Browser is your phone

Technology

Com Router is based on state of the art and open standards:

  • RESTful services: twilio, bulksms
  • Awesome Stack: node.js (nginx, ubuntu)
  • UI: bootstrap, jquery
